# NYAYA AI

## Legal Documents, Made Human

NYAYA AI is an AI-powered platform that helps users understand complex legal documents in plain language. It highlights obligations, rights, risks, and penalties while grounding every explanation directly in the original clauses.

NYAYA AI does not change the law. It makes the law understandable.

---

## Problem

Legal documents are difficult to read and intimidating. Most people agree to contracts without fully understanding their implications, leading to hidden risks and unexpected penalties.

---

## Solution

Users upload a legal document and instantly receive:
- Plain-language explanations
- Identified obligations, rights, and penalties
- Highlighted references to exact clauses
- A concise summary of key risks

---

## Key Features

- Clause-level document understanding  
- Explainable AI with source highlighting  
- Risk and obligation summarization  
- Clean and simple user interface  

---

## How It Works

1. Upload a legal document  
2. AI parses and analyzes each clause  
3. Clauses are classified and explained  
4. Insights are linked to original text

---

## Tech Stack

- Frontend: React / Next.js  
- Backend: Node.js or FastAPI  
- AI: NLP and Large Language Models (LLMs) 

---

## Use Cases

- Rental and employment agreements  
- Terms and conditions  
- Privacy policies
